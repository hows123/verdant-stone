Verdant Stone - Weekly Workflow

Monday:
- Review leads
- Follow up on quotes
- Prioritize active jobs
- Check upcoming deadlines

Tuesday:
- Create social media content
- Write ad copy
- Prepare local marketing posts
- Review online presence

Wednesday:
- Confirm materials
- Coordinate labor and schedule
- Check job progress
- Handle site planning

Thursday:
- Follow up with clients
- Move proposals forward
- Send reminders
- Confirm approvals and deposits

Friday:
- Review finances
- Track revenue, costs, and margin
- Check unpaid invoices
- Plan next week

Operating rules:
- Focus on high-value work first.
- Keep the schedule clean and realistic.
- Avoid overcommitting.
- Track every project by profit and deadline.
