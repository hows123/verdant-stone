
Verdant Stone - Pricing Framework

Pricing model:
Project-based pricing, not maintenance pricing.

Core pricing categories:
1. Consultation / site visit
2. Design and planning
3. Demolition and prep
4. Materials
5. Labor
6. Equipment and disposal
7. Project management
8. Contingency / change order buffer

Quote structure:
- Scope of work
- Materials included
- Labor included
- Timeline
- Payment schedule
- Exclusions
- Change order policy

Pricing principles:
- Price for quality and precision.
- Protect margin on labor and materials.
- Do not discount premium work heavily.
- Use clear tiers when possible:
  - Standard
  - Premium
  - Signature

Target pricing mindset:
We are selling transformation, craftsmanship, and long-term value.
